Purpose and Mission

In 2017 there was a massive campaign for suicide prevention and awareness called, You're Worth It. In the following year it spread from prevention and awareness to being a massive resource for mental health care. The Website had some amazing forums filled with tons of testimonials, book recommendations, support groups, meet-up schedules by city and state, and tons of other incredible things. At some point in 2020 the website was removed, this affected me and im sure many others pretty hard. My aim with this is to recreate that resource and environment for those in need. Suicide is the result of invalidating mental illness and problems. Whatever your struggle may be it is valid and you matter.

  • Suicide is the third leading cause of death for teens and young adults, ages 15-24 (SAVE.ORG 2021)
  • 1 in 3 (30.6%) young adults between the ages of 18 and 25 experienced a mental, behavioral, or emotional health issue in the past year (SAMHSA, 2021)
  • Nearly 800,000 people die by suicide in the world each year, which is roughly one death every 40 seconds (Global Statistics 2021)
  • The second highest rate in 2020 was among American Indian/Alaska Native (14.6 per 100,000)
  • An estimated 275,000 each year become suicide survivors

Every flower must grow through the dirt, and this world is more beautiful because of it!
